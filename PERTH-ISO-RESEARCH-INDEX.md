## Success Factors for ISO/Systems Auditing Sector Outreach

1. **Specialization Knowledge:** Demonstrate understanding of ISO standards (9001, 14001, 45001, 27001)
2. **Audit Framework Credibility:** Speak their language (internal audit, external audit, gap analysis, compliance mapping)
3. **Compliance Context:** Address pain points (audit readiness, certification costs, timeline pressure, auditor training needs)
4. **Systems-First Positioning:** Emphasize systems integration and holistic compliance approach, not just single-standard consulting
5. **Complementary Service Positioning:** Position as partner adding value, not competitor threatening their market
6. **Long-Term Relationship Focus:** Compliance/audit is trust-based business; emphasize strategic partnership, not transactional engagement
7. **Decision-Maker Credibility:** Target director/principal consultant; demonstrate equivalent expertise level
8. **Value-First Opening:** Lead with industry insights, not product pitch; use diagnostic/advisory framing
